Our Step-by-Step Project Bid Process

We follow a clear and structured process to ensure successful project delivery and client satisfaction. Here's how we manage each phase of the project journey:

1. Initial Inquiry & Consultation

We begin with an inquiry from you, where we understand your project requirements. This includes a consultation to discuss your vision, goals and any specific preferences, ensuring we fully understand your needs.

2. Requirement Analysis & Solution Proposal

After gathering your input, we analyze the requirements and propose the best solution for your project. This will include selecting the right technologies, features and setting realistic timelines for achieving the desired results.

3. Project Proposal Submission

We provide a detailed project proposal, which outlines the scope of work, deliverables, timeline and an itemized cost estimate. This ensures transparency and sets clear expectations for both parties.

4. Agreement Finalization

Once the proposal is reviewed, we finalize the agreement, which will include:

  • Payment Terms: This covers both advance payments and monthly payments. The advance payment allows us to begin the project, while the monthly payments cover ongoing services like server management, API integration and maintenance.
  • Confidentiality and Data Protection: These clauses ensure the protection of your data throughout the project lifecycle.

5. Advance Payment

An advance payment is required to start the project. Once the payment is received, we initiate the planning and development phase, starting with a detailed project roadmap.

6. Monthly Payment for Ongoing Services

Once the project is underway, monthly payments will be required for ongoing services such as:

  • Server and Hosting Management
  • API Integrations
  • Maintenance & Support
  • Additional Features or Enhancements

These monthly payments are payable between the 2nd and 5th of each month, based on the agreed terms. They ensure continued support, updates and any additional requirements needed during the development phase.

7. Project Development & Execution

We begin development according to the project plan, providing regular updates on milestones. We work closely with you to ensure all feedback is addressed as we move through the development stages.

8. Testing & Final Delivery

Before delivering the final product, we conduct rigorous testing to ensure it meets all requirements. Once you approve the solution, we deliver the final version to you.
